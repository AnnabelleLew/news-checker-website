Download

As of right now, the News Checker extension is still in development, so downloads can only be made directly from this website.

Firefox

Click here to download the extension for Firefox. Once downloaded, go to about:debugging, click on "Load Temporary Add-on", and click on the zip file in order to use it.

Chrome

Click here to download the extension for Chrome. Once downloaded, unzip the file, go to chrome://extensions, click on "Load unpacked", and click on the unzipped folder in order to use it.
