About Us

Mission

The News Checker Project was created to encourage news readers to have a deeper understanding of what they read. Given the importance of literacy and education in maintaining democracy, it's important to be aware of both implicit and intentional bias in news articles. I'm hoping that users will find this a useful tool to help them read the news thoughtfully.

Current Progress

As of right now, the News Checker Political Bias AI is in the training/debugging stages, as well as debugging the web extension. If you would like to contribute to training the AI, please download the extension here and make a user account here. If you would like to view the code behind the AI, click here to go to the GitHub repository.

Terms of Service

News Checker keeps track of the following data:

  • Article biases submitted by the user
  • The username associated with the article bias
  • The email address used to register for an account
    •

    User accounts are being used to ensure that the data collected is not being purposefully skewed by individual users. User emails will be kept private and used exclusively to notify users of project updates. News Checker only collects data that users submit through the web extension, and the data collected will only be used for News Checker projects. Users will be notified and given the option to opt out before any data is sent to third parties. Cookies need to be enabled for the extension to work, since the extension uses cookies to check for which user is logged in. News Checker holds the right to terminate user accounts for any reason. If you believe your account has been terminated by mistake, please email me at annabelle.lew02@gmail.com.
